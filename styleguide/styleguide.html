Introduction

Digital Scholarship Center and Services is part of University of Oregon Libraries at University of Oregon. The University of Oregon maintains an online style guide through communications.uoregon.edu. This style guide complements that while refining it for purposes of the library here and the Digital Scholarship Center in it.

Questions about this style guide can be directed to digitalscholarship@uoregon.edu.

The DSC is located in and administered by the UO Libraries, in partnership with multiple stakeholders and a Council of faculty advisors.
